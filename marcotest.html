The LBL is here
Marco So, Senior writer
April 30 2024
The Lunchtime basketball league is a fun basketball league played at lunchtime founded by Y10 students Will Law and Long Au Yeung, after a successful season last year, with the Chefs coming out on top, the LBL is looking at a exciting return to some competitive basketball. Despite coming off an eventful season, the LBL is looking forward to a big rebuild, creating 3 teams instead of two, appointing coaches and GMs, and even creating an inclusive womens league! In a surprising turn of events, The Reigning champion Chefs have undergone a remarkable transformation this season, emerging as a dynamic team. Spearheading this remarkable change is their appointed leader, Marco, with his formidable lineup of talented individuals, including Adam, a quick and swift playmaker, Kelly, a Canadian giant famous for his size, Preston, A rookie, excited to prove his dominance, Tiger, a strong bulldozing slasher, and last but not least, Nami, who is a stretch big with a long wingspan to block shots. With their combined skills and unwavering dedication, The Chefs are poised to make a significant impact in the upcoming competitions. Marco and Nami are likely to be a strong duo, being a deadly pick and roll threat for the respective opposing teams. Fans and critics alike eagerly await to witness the magic this squad can create.
There is a new team in the LBL! The wolves, led by Noah, whose exceptional leadership skills have brought a renewed sense of determination to the court this season. Alongside him, a talented roster of teammates has been assembled, including players such as Long, Caleb, Alexei, David, and Curtis. With their combined skills and unwavering teamwork, the Wolves are poised to dominate the competition. Noah and Long should serve as a powerful 1-2 punch for the rest of the teams, the Wolves are definitely a team to watch out for in this long awaited season.
Last but not least, the Alphas, starring Will Law, Darren Chung, and Callum Ng, many fans say this big 3 will be the most difficult star studded team to beat. This team also includes Dev Heera, Ian Mak, and Josiah Koh. This three role players are also crucial to keep this team afloat, Dev has remarkable sharpshooting, Ian is quick and flashy, and Josiah is long and bouncy. The collective skill and athleticism the Alphas have prove that they are a formidable group for the Chefs and the Wolves to take on.
Watch out for the LBL! You can catch them every wednesday at basketball courts, come watch! “We are waiting for the fans to turn up, who is going to win fan of the year?” asks player Marco So. Don't miss out on the chance to be part of the LBL's exciting journey and witness the thrilling moments that will surely unfold on the court.
